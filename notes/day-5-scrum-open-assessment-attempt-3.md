## Key Takeaways

- **Definition of Done** is the measure — not "filling the Sprint"
- **Sprints end at the timebox**, not after the Retrospective
- **The purpose of a Sprint** is to produce a done, useful, valuable Increment — this is a foundational truth
- **Scrum Master teaches, facilitates, and coaches** — they don't control the Sprint Backlog
- **Self-management** is non-negotiable — outside interference undermines Scrum
