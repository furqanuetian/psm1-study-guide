## Key Takeaways

- Sprints are fixed-length (≤ 1 month)
- A Sprint ends when the timebox expires — not when all tasks are done
- The Sprint Retrospective is the **last event**, but the Sprint itself ends at the timebox boundary
- Only the Product Owner can end a Sprint prematurely (if Sprint Goal becomes obsolete)
