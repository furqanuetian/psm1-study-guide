## Key Takeaways

| Concept | Correct Understanding |
|---------|----------------------|
| **Sprint Goal** | Outcome/value, not quality criteria |
| **Sprint Retrospective** | Scrum Team only — no stakeholders |
| **Scrum Master** | Coaches self-management, removes impediments |
| **Product Owner** | Maximizes value, manages backlog, collaborates with stakeholders |
| **Multiple teams** | Share Product Goal, Backlog, PO, DoD — but not necessarily Sprint start dates |
| **Sprint Review** | Happens regardless of whether Increment is Done |
