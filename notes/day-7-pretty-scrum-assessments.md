## Key Takeaways

### Sprint Goal vs. Product Goal
- **Sprint Goal** = single objective for the Sprint
- **Product Goal** = longer-term target (one at a time)
- **Definition of Done** = quality criterion, not an objective
- **Sprint Backlog** = plan and selected work, not the singular objective

### Outcome vs. Output
| Metric Type | Examples |
|-------------|----------|
| **Outcomes (Value)** | Time-to-Market, Customer satisfaction |
| **Outputs (Activity)** | Velocity, Scope delivered, Team productivity |

### Progress Monitoring
- Progress toward the **Product Goal** is monitored by the **entire Scrum Team** — not just the Product Owner
