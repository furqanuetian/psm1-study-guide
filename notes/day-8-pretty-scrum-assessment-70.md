## Key Takeaways

### Timeboxed Events
| Event | Timebox |
|-------|---------|
| Sprint | ≤ 1 month |
| Sprint Planning | Max 8 hours (1-month Sprint) |
| Daily Scrum | 15 minutes |
| Sprint Review | Max 4 hours (1-month Sprint) |
| Sprint Retrospective | Max 3 hours (1-month Sprint) |

### Definition of Done
- Updated during the **Sprint Retrospective** — not Sprint Planning
- The whole Scrum Team agrees on changes

### Scrum Master Role
- Coaches the team to improve skills, tools, and infrastructure
- Helps define an achievable Definition of Done
- Removes impediments
- Does NOT pause Sprints or lower quality
